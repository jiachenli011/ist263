The Bund

Walk along the iconic waterfront and enjoy views of historic architecture and the modern skyline.

Yu Garden

Explore the tranquility of Ming Dynasty gardens, pavilions, and koi-filled ponds.

Lujiazui

Marvel at futuristic skyscrapers like the Oriental Pearl Tower and Shanghai Tower.

Tianzifang

Stroll through narrow alleys filled with art galleries, cafes, and boutique shops.

Chongming Island

An eco-friendly escape featuring birdwatching, cycling trails, and lush greenery.

Nanjing Road

Experience Shanghai's bustling shopping street with local and global brands.
