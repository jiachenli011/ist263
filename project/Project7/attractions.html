Shanghai Attractions

Explore Shanghai’s Highlights

Shanghai seamlessly blends historical landmarks with cutting-edge modernity. Discover its most iconic attractions and make unforgettable memories.

The Bund

A riverside promenade offering breathtaking views of Shanghai’s historical and modern skyline. Best enjoyed at sunset.

Yu Garden

A Ming Dynasty classical garden with tranquil ponds, ancient architecture, and vibrant markets nearby.

Lujiazui

The heart of Shanghai’s financial district, featuring futuristic skyscrapers and observation decks.

Travel Tips

  • Early mornings at Yu Garden are less crowded.
  • The Bund is best visited at night for its dazzling light show.
  • Chongming Island is ideal for nature lovers looking for an escape from the city.
